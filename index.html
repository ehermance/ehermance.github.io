My Story

I'm Ellen Hermance, a web producer and product owner with 20 years of experience keeping enterprise websites healthy. At World Travel Holdings I managed web production across 30+ consumer brand sites and led a CMS migration spanning 18 of them, defining governance and building the reusable component systems that kept everything consistent and maintainable.

I'm the founder of PlantCareAI, a live AI-powered plant care platform I built by directing Claude Code as the development engine, and I write the plant care guides there myself. I'm also the voice behind Elloquist, a research-driven video channel exploring the science of everyday life, produced through Elloquist LLC. I wrote honestly about what "built it solo" means when you work with AI.

Outside of work, I care for my plants, cherish time with my family, and enjoy eating out, traveling, and exploring new ideas. These interests shape how I approach my work: attentive to details, open to discovery, and grounded in what matters most for people.
